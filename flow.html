Less Visited verifies and publishes the journey

The production workflow would combine the intake database, structured AI generation, human editing, official-source checks and versioned publication. The traveller then receives one personal link.

  • Named places and realistic neighbourhood grouping
  • Opening, access and booking checks with recheck dates
  • Alternatives for weather, energy and closures
  • Journey version, approval status and change requests
  • Offline access with live maps kept external
